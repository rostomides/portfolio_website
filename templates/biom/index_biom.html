Microbiome Visualizer v 1.0

Microbiome Visualizer is an interactive web application that allows to produce charts from Operational Taxonomic Units (OTU) tables. Its current version is optimized for Greengenes taxonomy . Microbiome Visualizer has also a feature allowing to predict if a sample is likely to belong to a patient with IBD. This feature is experimental and can by no mean be used for diagnostic purpose.
