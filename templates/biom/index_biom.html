Microbiome Visualizer v 1.0

Microbiome Visualizer is an intercative web application that allows to produce charts from Operational Taxonomic Units (OTU) tables. It is optimized for Greengenes taxonomy and for OTU tables generated using QIIME 1.9 pipeline. Microbiome Visualizer has also a feature allowing to predict if a samples is likely to belong to a patient with IBD. Of course this feature is an experimental one and is by no mean a diagnostic tool. Larbi Bedrani is not responsible of any outcome resulting from the usage of the predictions of this feature.
