LARBI BEDRANI

Hi, My name is Larbi Bedrani

I am an enthusiastic researcher, bioinformatician and data scientist who through his career enjoyed solving problems related to the healthcare system, human disease, livestock breeding and pathology and food microbiology both in business and academic environments. Rich of this variety of experiences, I learned how to approach my fields of expertise "the classical way" and also by thinking outside the box always striving to innovate. Passionate, curious, focused and dedicated are words that qualify me the best. The most valuable lesson I learned from life: "don't focus too much on the destination, focus more on finding the best way to reach it and of course enjoy the journey!".

My Projects

List of ongoing projects I am working on or already finished ...

Biom Visualizer is a web application that allows the user to interactively visualize Operational Taxonomic Units (OTUs) tables. It is optomized for GreenGenes taxonomy.

  • Current features
  • Load OTU Tables
  • Visualize the different taxonomic levels of a single sample
  • Compare several samples at all available taxonomic levels
  • Upcoming features
  • Predict if the microbial profiles input by the user are similar to Inflammatory Bowel Diseases profiles. The predictive model is currently in development and uses the power of machine learning on publically available 16S sequencing datasets of human gut microbiome.
